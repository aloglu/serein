---
title: I am Panting
author: Anna Swir
translator: Czesław Miłosz
date: 2026-03-27
---

Why talk
if one can shout
why walk
if one can run
why live
if one can burn.

I am running and screaming from joy
I am running and screaming from despair
I am panting
my lungs work like crazy.

Violent feelings
are good, so I have heard, for your health.
