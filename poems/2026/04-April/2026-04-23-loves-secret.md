---
title: Love’s Secret
poet: William Blake
date: 2026-04-23
---

Never seek to tell thy love,
::line |~1ch| Love that never told can be;
For the gentle wind doth move
::line |~1ch| Silently, invisibly.

I told my love, I told my love,
::line |~1ch| I told her all my heart,
Trembling, cold, in ghastly fears.
::line |~1ch| Ah! she did depart!

Soon after she was gone from me,
::line |~1ch| A traveller came by,
Silently, invisibly:
::line |~1ch| He took her with a sigh.
