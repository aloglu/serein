---
title: Untitled
poet: James Baldwin
date: 2026-07-19
---

Lord,
::line |~5ch| when you send the rain
::line |~5ch| think about it, please,
::line |~5ch| a little?

::line |~2ch| Do
::line |~5ch| not get carried away
::line |~5ch| by the sound of the falling water,
::line |~5ch| the marvelous light
::line |~5ch| on the falling water.

::line |~2ch| I
::line |~5ch| am beneath that water.
::line |~5ch| It falls with great force
::line |~5ch| and the light

::line |~2ch| Blinds
::line |~5ch| me to the light.
