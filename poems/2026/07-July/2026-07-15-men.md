---
title: Men
poet: Dorothy E. Reid
date: 2026-07-15
---

I like men.
::line |~3ch| They stride about,
They reach in their pockets
::line |~3ch| And pull things out;

They look important,
::line |~3ch| They rock on their toes,
They lose all the buttons
::line |~3ch| Off of their clothes;

They throw away pipes,
::line |~3ch| They find them again.
Men are queer creatures;
::line |~3ch| I like men.
